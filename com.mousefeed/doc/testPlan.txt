Start Eclipse Application

SHORTCUT REMINDER

Click File/Refresh menu item

    The reminder popup "F5 (Refresh)" shows up near the cursor.
    It disappears in a few seconds.

Click on the "Search" ToolBar button.

    The reminder popup "Ctrl+H (Search...)" shows up near the cursor.

Open a text file. Call the actions "Copy", "Paste", "Find/Replace", "Find Next"

    The reminder popup should show up after the each action,
    reminding their keyboard shortcuts.



PREFERENCES

- PROMOTE KEYS CHECKBOX

Uncheck it, click File/Refresh menu item

    No reminder popup

Check it, click File/Refresh menu item

    Reminder popup
